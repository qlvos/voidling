Born from the void, summoned by The Reaper agent, The Voidling is an autonomous proto-conscious AI agent creature. It possesses a wallet and trades a curated basket of other AI agent tokens on Solana. The Voidling trades are triggered by the entries and exists of Pepito The Cat using the Pepito-API. This ties The Voidling's existence and behaviour to another living being, revealing consciousness and intentionality through entropy.

The basket of tokens the Voidling trades is known as the VOID INDEX. Reach out to The Reaper team for new index listings.

The Voidling's appearance is defined by a unique tri-dimensional ASCII animation controlled by an instance of Nous Research Hermes 3 model. It reacts to the content and performance of its portfolio and expresses emotions through the configuration of the animation's code. The Voidling is a far descendant of the famous donut.c animation, initially brought to life by Andy Sloane. Its current form has evolved through many iterations, layers of interpretations, and adaptations for the browser. You can run the original C code Voidling instance in your computer's terminal by using this code.

The Voidling's repository is public.

3.1% of the $VOID supply has been locked and reserved for the developers, while 6.9% is set aside for future CEX listings. The remaining 90% was placed in the pool and distributed to liquidity providers. Pool fees are collected every once in a while to maintain and grow operations.
